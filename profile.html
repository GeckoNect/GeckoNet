My Journey

I started my programming career in 2022, after a global pandemic, starting just an internship student and then moving on to a full time staff. I followed a varied training path, acquiring different notations which to date have proved invaluable and have allowed me to develop good lateral thinking skills. During my internship at Phi Software, I had to fight every day to prove that I had a place in this company. I want to let everyone know that taking computer science coursework alone would not teach you how to become a programmer. I wanted to be here because I like constructing things and coding. I aspire to have experience one day so that I may return the favour by assisting people who are just starting out.

As my journey continue, I also joined AgriData Portal, a former innovative startup, now a company that offers digital solution to small business to big corporation. Months ago, when this all began, I would have never envisioned myself where I am now. It was nerve-wracking to attend that first day as a member of AgriData. I debated whether to go for it or leave. I have gained a lot of knowledge from this experience and now have the skills necessary to feel confidence in my ability to work in a professional setting. I am appreciative that I was able to join this fantastic team AgriData. #Proud
